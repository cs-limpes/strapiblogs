About Me

I truly believe that technology is a tool that can make lives better, easier, and more productive - taking humanity to places we only ever dreamed of and I'm extremely excited and proud of my contributions to that development. That workers and employees often feel new software makes processes take longer and require more work is a terrible error in software design that could easily be rectified but it's so easily overlooked by most consultants.

Three things I stand behind:

I'm passionate about user experience and believe strongly in listening to the people using your product - external and internal. They know best how a product is being used rather than how it was intended to be used and it's essential to listen to their feedback and requests.

A truly intuitive user interface has yet to be developed - we have all been taught to use websites and applications and because they all use similar interfaces, they "feel" intuitive - but they aren't.

Accessibility is essential and the curb cut effect is real. If you ignore disabled and otherwise impaired users, your product will suffer and you will be left behind - this user group needs to be top of your list instead of an after thought.
